About Mazwai

Mazwai was built with one simple aim: to provide free, high quality, cinematic style stock footage, that can be used in a wide-range of creative projects. All of our royalty-free video clips have been hand-picked by our team of video experts, brining you an unrivaled selection of quality visual content with simple, and most importantly, safe licensing.

We only work directly with selected artists, which means you can be safe in the knowledge that every video clip on Mazwai comes with all the necessary permissions to be used in your own projects, on a royalty-free basis.

If you would like to submit your own free video clips to Mazwai, we would love to work with you! Please drop us a line at hello@mazwai.com and we'll get the ball rolling.
